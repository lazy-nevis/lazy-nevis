# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, accountability, and focus on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized attention, insults, threats, publishing private information, and other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report conduct privately to the maintainer through the contact method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), marking it as a conduct report. The maintainer may correct, warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participation according to context and will protect reporter privacy as far as possible.
