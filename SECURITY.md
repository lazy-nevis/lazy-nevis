# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

Until `1.0.0`, only the latest stable release receives security fixes. Prereleases are for evaluation and may be replaced without backports.

## Reporting

Use GitHub's **Report a vulnerability** private advisory for this repository. If that feature is unavailable, contact the maintainer through the private address listed on [sims.dev.br](https://sims.dev.br) and include `LazyNevis security` in the subject. Do not open a public issue, discussion, or PR for an unpatched vulnerability. Never include credentials, private keys, personal session exports, or other people's data.

Expect acknowledgment within 5 business days and an initial assessment within 10 business days. Remediation and disclosure timing depend on severity and platform coordination. Good-faith research that avoids privacy violations, persistence, service disruption, and data destruction is welcome.
